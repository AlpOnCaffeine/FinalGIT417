About the Artist

Timothy(Moth) Pease is a ceramics and steel sculptor from the Azores, Portugal. His works weaves together a rich tapestry of resources and experiences, exploring history and celebrating the power of beauty, transformation, and purpose. "As a U.S. veteran, I create art for healing and hope it brings the same to those who view it." - Timothy Pease
